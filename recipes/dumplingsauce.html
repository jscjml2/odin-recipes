Dumpling Sauce Recipe

Chinese dumpling sauce, made with simple ingredients

This is just basically a simple recipe for some dumpling sauce...

It goes well with any type of dumpling, and is quick and easy to make.

Ingredients:

  • 1 Tablespoon of Soy Sauce
  • 1 Teaspoon of Sesame Oil

Recipe Instructions:

  1. Mix both ingredients together
  2. Enjoy with dumplings
